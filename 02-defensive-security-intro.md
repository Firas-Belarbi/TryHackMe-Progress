## ðŸ”‘ Key Concepts Learned

### 1. What is Defensive Security?
- Protecting systems from unauthorized access  
- Monitoring networks and logs for signs of attacks  
- Responding to incidents quickly to minimize damage  
- Implementing security controls and policies  

### 2. Roles in the Blue Team
**SOC Analyst (Tier 1 / Tier 2):**  
- Monitors SIEM alerts  
- Investigates suspicious events  
- Escalates real threats to incident responders  

**Threat Hunter:**  
- Proactively searches for undetected threats  
- Looks for abnormal patterns and attacker behavior  

**Incident Responder:**  
- Handles active security incidents  
- Contains attacks, removes malware, and restores systems
